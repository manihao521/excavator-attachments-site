How to choose the right attachment

1. Machine

Confirm excavator brand, model, tonnage and hydraulic flow.

2. Interface

Measure pin diameter, arm width and pin center distance.

3. Jobsite

Describe rock, concrete, steel, pile type, trench or compaction condition.
