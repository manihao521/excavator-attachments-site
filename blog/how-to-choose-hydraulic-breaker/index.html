How to Choose a Hydraulic Breaker for Your Excavator

Choose by excavator weight, hydraulic oil flow, working pressure, chisel diameter, bracket type and material to be broken. For hard rock and quarry work, confirm impact energy and spare parts support before ordering.

Information to prepare before quotation

  • Excavator brand, model and tonnage
  • Attachment type and expected quantity
  • Working condition with photos or video
  • Destination country and delivery requirement
